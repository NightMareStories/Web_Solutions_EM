Google’s new Play Store section
07.05.2024 IT news

Google is rolling out a new section in Play Store listings that forces developers to explain what data they're collecting and how it's being used. The new section is titled 'Data safety' and will appear alongside the usual sections like 'About this app' and 'Ratings and reviews'. Apple released a similar feature as part of numerous privacy-enhancing features in last year's iOS 14. Google showed off its version last year but is only beginning to roll it out this week
